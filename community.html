Community at Texas A&M

Classes

In your classes, you will meet several peers and make new friends. Try to put yourself out there, help each other out, and ask questions.
Some of the people you meet in these classes could become your closest friends throughout your time here, so make the most of it!

Organizations

There are more than 1,000 student organizations on campus.
Getting involved with an organization or club can help you meet people with similar
interests that are outside of your classes. If you don't see an organization that's for you, you can start a new one!

Intramural Sports

Dozens of intramural sports are offered at Texas A&M and you can join with a group of friends, or even a random team.
This is a great way to stay active and take a break from your studies. Intramurals usually take place in the evening times and
there are competitive and non-competitive sports so that anyone can play!

Traditions

Texas A&M is full of traditions and this is a big part of what makes the University so unique.
Some common traditions include Silver Taps, Muster, Midnight Yell, Aggie Ring, Elephant walk, and many more.
If you don't participate in traditions, you may get called a 2 percenter!
