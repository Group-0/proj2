Study Spaces

On this page you can find many popular study spots for both on- and off-campus!


On-Campus

Evan's Library

Evan's Library sits in the middle of campus and offers many study areas throughout its many floors!
If you're looking for somewhere more private, Evan's also offers group and quiet individual study rooms you can book depending on availability!


The Annex

Located right next to Evan's is the Annex! Often overlooked is this great spot on-campus that also offers both an open study area and study rooms you can book a reservation for!


The Student Computing Center

Also known as the SCC, is a great place students often forget about. Not only can you find rows and rows of desks to work on, you can also utilize the many computers and printers it houses!


The MSC

The MSC offers many study spots, such as the flag room, in front of Barnes and Nobles Starbucks, etc!
