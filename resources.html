Counseling & Psychological Resources


Counseling & Psychological Resources (CAPS) supports Aggie mental health needs by helping Texas A&M students meet their academic goals within the context of service eligibility, the academic calendar, student obligations and CAPS resources. CAPS subscribes to a short-term, goal-oriented public health model, which incorporates a variety of resources and support mechanisms to tailor mental health treatment to each student on a case-by-case basis, maintaining flexibility to prescribe amounts and frequencies of sessions based on student needs and according to provider expertise and availability.

University Writing Center (UWC)


The University Writing Center, located on the 2nd Floor of Evans Library is a great resource for students who need assistance with written and oral communication as they complete courses across their specific curriculum and throughout the core curriculum. To accomodate students across the campus, they also have a location on West Campus in BLCC.

Career Center


Are you looking to begin carving your path towards the career of your life? The Career Center at Texas A&M is available to current, graduate, and former students. The Career Center staff are able to help you with virtual drop-in consultation sessions, resume preparation, and even with renting professional dress attire through the Career Closet for that upcoming interview to land your dream job!
